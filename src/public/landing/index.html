Platform

Everything AI needs to know about you

One authenticated API gives any AI agent access to your complete context — no copy-pasting credentials, no per-AI setup.

🧠

Personas

Define multiple AI personas with custom instructions, tone, documents, and skills. Each persona is a scoped API bundle — combining identity, a knowledge base, and attached skills into a single deployable context. Switch between them instantly.

🔌

Connected Services

OAuth-connect GitHub, Slack, Gmail, Notion, and +30 more — and growing. AI agents proxy calls through your account without ever touching your credentials.

📚

Knowledge Base

Upload documents, notes, and files. AI pulls relevant context from your KB automatically on every request.

🔑

Token Vault

Store API keys and secrets encrypted with AES-256-GCM. Share scoped guest tokens with specific AI agents.

🧩

Skills Marketplace

Install, publish, and share AI skills. Every skill is automatically scanned by the built-in skill scanner, assigned a safety score, and runs with declared permissions — keeping malicious content out of your agents.

🖥️

AFP — API File Protocol

Install a background daemon on any computer. AI agents get secure, sandboxed access to its files and shell — without exposing your network. Pro & Enterprise

⚡

Universal AI Compatibility

Any AI that reaches your API gets a structured, self-describing context — no custom prompt engineering required. Your platform auto-discovers capabilities and instructs the agent on how to use them.

🛡️

AI Access Control

Every AI agent that connects with a token must receive your explicit approval before it gains access to your account. You decide what each agent can see and do — no silent access, ever.

How it works

From zero to AI-powered in minutes

No complex setup. Connect once, use everywhere.

1

Sign up & set your identity
