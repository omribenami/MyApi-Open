# Privacy Policy — MyApi GPT

**Effective date:** 2026-03-31

## What this GPT does

This GPT connects to your MyApi account at **www.myapiai.com** to let you interact with your personal data through ChatGPT. It reads identity information, personas, knowledge base documents, and service connection status that you have previously stored in your MyApi account.

## Data access

- This GPT reads data from your MyApi account using an OAuth access token you authorize.
- The GPT does **not** store your data independently. All data remains in your MyApi account.
- The GPT does **not** write to or modify your MyApi account — it is read-only.
- Conversation content may be processed by OpenAI per their [Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy).

## OAuth token

- When you first use this GPT, you authorize it to access your MyApi account via OAuth.
- Your access token is stored by ChatGPT/OpenAI and used only to call the MyApi API on your behalf.
- You can revoke this access at any time from your MyApi dashboard under **Token Vault**.

## Data retention

- MyApi stores your data per our [Privacy Policy](https://www.myapiai.com/privacy).
- Revoke access in the MyApi dashboard → Token Vault → find the "ChatGPT (OAuth)" token → revoke.
